About Anne

Not a speaker who happens to know about resilience.

Anne Grady has lived this material. After years of navigating personal and professional adversity, she turned her experience into a science-backed, practical system — field-tested in boardrooms, conference halls, and leadership retreats across every major industry.

She's a bestselling author of three books, a two-time TEDx speaker, and has been featured on NBC, CBS, and in dozens of major business publications. Her clients include Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, healthcare systems, and nonprofit leaders who want real change — not motivational noise.
