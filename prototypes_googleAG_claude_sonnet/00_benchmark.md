## Outbest Targets for Anne Grady Group

1. **Clarity of offer within 3 seconds.** Every competitor takes 5–10 seconds to understand the core offer. AGG will state the category, the benefit, and the audience in the first viewport. Not a tagline — a promise.

2. **Proof above the fold, integrated, not listed.** Simon T. Bailey does this well with stats. AGG will go further by integrating a mini-proof bar (logos + a stat + a testimonial snippet) immediately below the hero — not in a separated logo band.

3. **Sticky mobile CTA with availability language.** No competitor does this. A persistent "Check Availability" pill on mobile captures planners who are scrolling to research, not just those who scroll to the bottom.

4. **Outcomes-first speaking topics.** Every competitor leads with topic titles ("Resilience," "Leadership," "Change Management"). AGG will lead with what the audience takes away. Planners book outcomes, not topics.

5. **Visual coherence without color chaos.** One background palette system, clearly defined section rhythm, no jarring color shifts. Sections feel like pages of the same book, not a patchwork.

---

## Design Thesis (Master)

A premium, spacious keynote speaker site that out-executes competitors on offer clarity, proof density, and mobile conversion by treating every section as a single persuasion unit — never more than one thought per scroll step.
